Who We Are

Mission Statement

Through innovative teaching methods, nurturing guidance, and a vibrant learning environment, we aim to inspire a generation of confident, compassionate, and creative individuals who are prepared to make a positive impact on the world.

Approach And Philosophy

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that education goes beyond academics. Alongside our comprehensive curriculum, we prioritize social-emotional development, fostering a sense of curiosity, empathy, and resilience in our students.

Our Programs


Cognitive Development:

This involves the development of thinking skills such as problem-solving, memory, attention, and understanding concepts like shapes, colors, numbers, and letters.


Language Development:

Our programs support language skills including listening, speaking, reading etc. We use conversations, storytelling, and exposure to books and other language-rich environments.


Social and Emotional Development:

We foster the ability to interact positively with others, develop friendships, understand and express emotions, and regulate behavior. We teach empathy, cooperation, and conflict resolution.


Creative Development:

We encourage creativity through activities like art, music, dance, and imaginative play. Children explore their interests, experiment with materials, and express themselves in different ways.


Physical Development:

Besides motor skills, FOG educare also supports overall physical health and well-being through activities such as outdoor play, exercise, healthy eating habits, and promoting good hygiene practices.


Independence and Self-Help Skills:

FOG educare helps children develop independence by teaching them how to do things for themselves, such as dressing, feeding, and taking care of personal belongings.
